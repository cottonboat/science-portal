Chapter 2: Energy

Energy is everywhere! Learn how energy is transferred, how it changes from one form to another, and how it helps living things live and grow.

Chapter 3: Materials

What are things made of? Discover different materials, how solids and liquids behave, and how materials can change in exciting ways.

Chapter 4: Earth and its habitats

What is happening beneath our feet? Discover the structure of the Earth, explore volcanoes and earthquakes, and learn about the different habitats where living things survive.

Chapter 5: Light

How do we see the world around us? Explore how light travels and reflects, discover why we have day and night, and investigate the role of light in our solar system.

Chapter 6: Electricity

Become an electricity investigator! Test different materials, discover conductors and insulators, and learn how circuits and switches help electrical devices work.
